# Lesson 01 - How Computers Think

## What is a Computer?

A programmable electronic device that processes raw data into meaningful information.

---

## What is Data?

Data is raw facts or information before processing.

Examples:
- Name
- Age
- Marks
- Images

---

## What is an Algorithm?

A step-by-step procedure for solving a problem.

Example:
1. Boil water
2. Add tea
3. Add sugar
4. Drink tea

---

## What is a Program?

A set of instructions written in a programming language.

---

## What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of a computer or machine to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.
